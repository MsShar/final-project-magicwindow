THE MAGIC WINDOW

The Secret

Long ago, hidden deep within a dark old castle, there was a large magical window at the end of a mystical corridor. It was said that who ever gazed out of the magic window, if they believe, their imagination would take them to enchanted places where dreams can come true. Each day a line of people waited to look through the magic window. People came from near and far in hopes of having all of th deirreams fulfilled.

One day, an old woman in a wheelchair arrived at the castle to peer through the magic window. She asked, “ Magic window, what do you see?” Suddenly, through the window, she saw a vision of herself young and lovely. She was dancing in a radiant garden moving about like a beautiful ballerina. The old woman became so joyful that her spirit lifted her out of the wheelchair. She left the castle singing and dancing to the song from her vision.
